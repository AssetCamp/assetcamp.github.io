Features

Unlimited Free Uploads

Get Paid Every Two Weeks

The current solutions have slow and frustrating payout cycles. We’ll pay you on Paypal every two weeks.

Awesome Community Support & Feedback

We’re here to make sure that you’re having a great experience. Moreover, we’re trying to build a product with a great feedback cycle so that you can take your skills to the next level.

Non-Exclusive

Feel free to post your content wherever you want. We’re not here to tell you what to do and what not to do, unlike some other people ;)

Royalty Free License

The standard license for 3D content on the internet. The royalty-free license applies to the use of the exact 3D content as submitted by the artist.

Focus on .fbx format

Most sites are very confusing for users because of the several dozen file formats. We think that the .fbx format is extremely versatile as it can be used in almost every application.
